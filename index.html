Our business is making your business none of our business.

Encryptic is an open-source note-taking application heavily focused on encryption. When you start writing, no one will know what you write except for you.


We don’t know and we don’t want to know. That’s between you and your work.



Being watched is for the birds.

How it works

When you use our site, you run the app directly through your browser. Anything you write is stored in your browser’s cache using industry standard encryption methods. This means it’s kept safe, and by default never even leaves your computer.

# Markdown

Encryptic uses the Markdown formatting syntax for its notes so you can spend more time writing, and less time worrying about making things *italic*.

# To-do list

Encryptic gives you the possibility to create to-do lists. We also included a progress bar so you can really see how much you have left to reach your goal.
